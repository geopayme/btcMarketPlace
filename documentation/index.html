Interfaces

PrivateKeyInterface This is a contract for the PrivaetKey portion of ECDSA.
PointInterface This is the contract for implementing Point, which encapsulates entities and operations over the points on the Elliptic Curve.
EcDHInterface This is the contract for implementing EcDH (EC Diffie Hellman).
SignatureInterface This is the contract for describing a signature used in ECDSA.
